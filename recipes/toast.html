TOAST RECIPE

Description

This is a very delicious dish that makes sense diiieeeee.

It is very easy to prepare. Continue on this page if you want to make this delicious food.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • A toaster
  • Any from of spread

Steps

  1. Place bread in toaster.
  2. Select temperature for desired crispiness
  3. Push down toast lever to begin toasting.
  4. After toast pops out, remover and put on plate.
  5. Apply the spread of your choice.
  6. Enjoy!
