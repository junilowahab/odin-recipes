PANCAKE RECIPE

Description

This is a very delicious dish that makes sense diiieeeee.

It is very easy to prepare. Continue on this page if you want to make this delicious food.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder
  • Oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. Pour 3 scoops of flour into a bowl.
  2. Pour 2 scoops of sugar into the bowl.
  3. Crack 2 eggs into the bowl.
  4. Add 4 pinches of salt into the mix.
  5. Add 3 table spoons of powdered milk into the bowl.
  6. Add quater a table spoon of baking powder into the bowl.
  7. Pour water into the bowl and mix till it becomes a smooth, thick, watery substance.
  8. Pour oil into the frying pan and let it spread to cover every part of the pan.
  9. Scoop part of the mix and pour gently into the pan.
  10. Let it stay for a minute the flip it over. After another minute take it out of the pan an put in plate.
  11. Continue this till all the mix is finished.
